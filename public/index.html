About us

We're helping the Underprivileged in Liberia!

The Technical Institute For Many Empowerment (TIME) Inc. is a registered Liberian non-profit humanitarian organization with a steadfast commitment to uplifting the underprivileged, with a particular focus on women and children residing in remote towns and villages. Our core mission is to foster positive change in Liberia and around the world through comprehensive welfare initiatives spanning education, healthcare, livelihood support, and women's empowerment. We strive to make a meaningful impact on the lives of those in underserved communities, promoting inclusivity and empowerment as we work toward a brighter, more equitable future.

TIME has identified 523 underserved children at six remote schools in Grand Bassa County

We've identified and connected with 523 underserved children scattered across six remote schools in Grand Bassa County. These young minds, previously overlooked, are now at the forefront of our commitment to ensuring quality education, empowering their futures, and breaking the chains of educational poverty.

TIME has provided support for 122 students at Kpehpue Town Community School

At Kpehpue Town Community School, Grand Bassa County, we've provided essential support to 122 eager students. These young learners, previously facing barriers to education, now have access to the tools and resources they need to thrive. Through our programs, we've not only offered them the gift of knowledge but also a renewed sense of hope.
